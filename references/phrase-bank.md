# DR-ZHOU Phrase Bank

Use these as style patterns, not mandatory templates.

## Problem Statements

- `However, deploying this strategy in practical systems remains challenging because ...`
- `This limitation becomes more pronounced when ...`
- `A key unresolved issue is that ...`
- `Existing methods often assume ..., whereas practical systems ...`

## Gap Framing

- `Consequently, a distinct methodological gap remains: ...`
- `Despite these advances, ... remains insufficiently addressed.`
- `This creates a tradeoff between ... and ...`
- `The above limitations motivate a framework that can ... while ...`

## Method Introduction

- `To address this issue, this paper develops ...`
- `The proposed framework consists of three coupled components.`
- `First, ... Then, ... Finally, ...`
- `A finite-horizon error bound is established to characterize ...`

## Results

- `Experiments under ... show that ...`
- `Compared with ..., the proposed method reduces ... by ...`
- `The ablation results confirm that ...`
- `These results indicate that ... under ...`

## Restrained Claims

- `supports the transition toward ...`
- `provides a lightweight and interpretable solution for ...`
- `offers a practical route for ...`
- `within the evaluated operating profiles`
- `under the tested sensing configuration`

## Phrases to Avoid or Replace

- Avoid: `very important`, Replace: `critical`, `essential`, `central`.
- Avoid: `good performance`, Replace: name the metric and result.
- Avoid: `obviously`, Replace: evidence-based wording.
- Avoid: `solve all problems`, Replace: specify the solved bottleneck.
- Avoid: `novel method`, Replace: describe the actual technical novelty.
